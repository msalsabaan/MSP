COMPANY / BRAND ASSETS
======================

Site-wide brand files referenced on the "Company & Contact" tab.

Suggested file names:
  logo-light.svg   (or .png)  - logo for light backgrounds
  logo-dark.svg    (or .png)  - logo for dark backgrounds
  favicon.png      (512 x 512) - browser tab icon
  hero.jpg         - homepage hero background (>= 1920 px wide)
  og-image.jpg     - social share image (1200 x 630)

Tips:
- SVG preferred for logos.
- Keep originals; do not overwrite with low-res versions.
