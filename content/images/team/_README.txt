TEAM MEMBER PHOTOS
==================

One photo per person. The file name must match the "Photo File" column
on the Team Members tab.

Naming rule (lowercase, hyphens, person's name):
  team-first-last.jpg     e.g. team-sara-alqahtani.jpg

Tips:
- Square crop works best (1:1), at least 800 x 800 px.
- Head-and-shoulders, consistent background across the whole team.
- JPG preferred.
